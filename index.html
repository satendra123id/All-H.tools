💡 Quick Professional Tips

1

Always Start with Recon

80% of successful pentests depend on good recon. Spend 60% of your time on information gathering before running any exploit.

2

Document Everything

Maintain detailed notes of every command, output, and finding. Use tools like CherryTree or KeepNote for organized reporting.

3

Master the CLI

GUI tools are slower. Learn command-line versions of tools — they're faster, scriptable, and work over SSH.

4

Stay Legal & Ethical

Always have written authorization. Use these tools only on systems you own or have explicit permission to test.
